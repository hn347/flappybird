Results

The results of the project are best evaluated by actually playing the game on the RP2040 and VGA setup. The game should feel responsive when the player flaps, the animation should look smooth and stable at a steady frame rate, collisons and scoring should behave consistently, and the audio feedback should be immediate and correct.

We implemented everything that was proposed in our final project demo. In our final implementation, this included the players being able to select between the three control modes: an arcase button flap, microphone-triggered flap, or IMU tilt flap.
